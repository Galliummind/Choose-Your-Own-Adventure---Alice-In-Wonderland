The king's Jungle was actually a maze! It might as well be a labyrinth, each turn seems sharp and unnatural..."How will I get through?" Alice thought to herself. Three sign's stood in front of her 'Queen's Garden, North, East, West.' But none of them told Alice which way to the queen. Alice surveyed the area, but there wasn't a soul to be found. "I guess I will just need to wander until I find the queen" Alice said to no one.
