Alice walked what seemed like forever always keeping her bearing East. Suddenely Alice couldn't walk East any longer, a solid wall the span of ten men, plus two stood in her way. Alice looked to her left...then her right. "It seems I have reached a dead end." She said to no one. She looked around her, the walls were made of crude stone bricks. upon the walls grew a massive vine of yellow flowers. Alice put her hand against the thicket and was suprised when her hands passed through. The vines were too thick to see what lay ahead...But Alice thought she might be able to push through...
