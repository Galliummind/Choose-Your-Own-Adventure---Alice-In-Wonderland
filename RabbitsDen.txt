Alice entered into a clearing, the space well manicured and every square inch of the place was pruned and mantained. This must be the Rabbit's Den! Before Alice could take another step the White-Rabbit entered the clearing. "I'm late, I'm LATE! For a very important date." The rabbit shouted as he ran frantically in and out of the den. Each time he emerged he seemed to be holding something new. 'What an unorganized rabbit.' Alice thought to herself. The Rabbit seemed pretty busy, Alice wondered if she knew where the queen's crown may be.
