This is an eerie place, spiderwebs spell out directions of where to go in cruel writin; "MAD HATTER, DON"T GO THIS WAY!" another web says "TWEEDLE DEE AND TWEEDLE DUM LIKE CAKE!" and another: "RABBIT'S DEN". After what seems like an endless amount of messages written on the wall Alice simply decides that the only way to get to the Queen is to pick a path and head in that direction.
